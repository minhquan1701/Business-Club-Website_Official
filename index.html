We Are

Established in 2004, Business Club (BC) is a RMIT student's academic club that is of a connective bridge between students and companies, enabling them to experience the real working environment. With 16 years of experience, BC has proudly organized one annual event for RMIT community,two nationwide competitions and many other projects for the Vietnamese youth.

why

We Do

Vision A professional environment where talents are created and empowered. Mission To support young talents, who wish to develop themselves, via national-scale projects, big-scale events within a professional working environment.
