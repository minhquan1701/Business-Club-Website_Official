who

We Are

Business Club (BC) is a RMIT student's academic club established since 2004. We are here to be a connective bridge between RMIT students and companies, enabling them to experience the real working environment. Operating with 3 departments: Marketing, Human Resources, Research and Development, BC has organized 10 seasons of Marketing Challengers since 2009, RMIT Vietnam Teacher's Day and 6 seasons of The Logisticom since 2004.

why

We Do

Vision A professional environment where talents are created and empowered. Mission To support young talents, who wish to develop themselves, via national-scale projects, big-scale events within a professional working environment.
